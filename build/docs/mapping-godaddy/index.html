    Mapping Domain from GoDaddy

    This section covers how to map a domain hosted or purchased on GoDaddy to Hashnode.

    1. Create an account on GoDaddy.

    1. Purchase your preferred domain.

    2. After completing the purchase, you will be redirected to the product page. Here, you will see your domain.

    1. On the domain card, click on the Kebab menu icon and select Manage DNS.

    1. Click on the Add button on the DNS management page to add the A record on your domain.

    1. Select A as the type, and add @ or blog as the host. Finally, point it to 192.241.200.144.

    1. Click on Save.
